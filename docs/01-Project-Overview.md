## Overview
HealthPal is a comprehensive digital healthcare platform designed to support
Palestinians in accessing medical care during crises and healthcare system
limitations. The platform connects patients, doctors, donors, volunteers,
and medical NGOs in a single, secure digital ecosystem.

HealthPal focuses on enabling remote medical services, transparent medical
sponsorship, medication and equipment coordination, psychological support,
and public health awareness.

---

## Problem Statement
In many crisis-affected regions, access to healthcare is limited due to:
- Shortage of medical facilities and specialists
- Lack of transparency in medical donations
- Difficulty coordinating medicines and equipment
- Limited access to psychological support
- Poor dissemination of public health information

HealthPal addresses these challenges by providing a centralized platform
that enables coordination, transparency, and secure communication between
all stakeholders.

---

## Project Goals
- Enable remote medical consultations regardless of location
- Ensure transparency and accountability in medical donations
- Coordinate medical supplies efficiently and safely
- Provide secure psychological support services
- Improve public health awareness through alerts and education
- Maintain high standards of security, privacy, and access control

---

## Target Users
- Patients seeking medical care or psychological support
- Doctors and counselors providing healthcare services
- Donors sponsoring medical treatments
- NGOs managing aid operations and inventory
- Volunteers delivering medications and equipment
- Administrators supervising platform operations
