## Overview
Feature 6 manages verified NGOs, medical missions, and coordinated healthcare
campaigns such as mobile clinics and surgical missions.

---

## Core Components

### 1. Verified NGO Network
- Add and manage NGOs
- Fetch all registered organizations
- Update organization information
- Ensures only verified organizations participate

### 2. Mission Scheduling
- Doctors define availability
- Patients submit appointment requests
- Doctors approve or reject appointments
- Both doctors and patients can view appointments
- Appointments can be canceled

### 3. Surgical Missions Tracker
- Create and manage surgical missions
- Display upcoming missions
- View mission details
- Register patients for missions
- Medical teams approve or reject registrations
- View registered patients list

---

## Key Design Highlights
- Verification of organizations
- Controlled approval workflows
- Clear separation of responsibilities
- Support for coordinated medical outreach

---

## Roles Involved
- NGO Staff
- Doctor
- Patient
- Admin
