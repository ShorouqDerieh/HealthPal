## Overview
Feature 4 focuses on improving community health awareness and timely
communication through educational content, public health alerts, and webinars.

---

## Problem Addressed
In crisis situations, lack of accurate health information and delayed alerts
can cause serious harm. This feature ensures reliable dissemination of
health education and urgent notifications.

---

## Core Modules

### 1. Health Guides
- Users can browse health guides
- Optional filtering by category or search
- Admins can create, edit, and delete guides
- Guides support HTML content for rich presentation

### 2. Public Health Alerts
- Alerts include severity, region, source, and expiry
- Public users can view alerts
- Admins can filter alerts by status, region, severity, and date
- Users can mark alerts as read and view unread alerts

### 3. Notification Preferences
- Users control notification settings:
  - Email enabled / disabled
  - Preferred language (default Arabic)

### 4. Workshops & Webinars
- Create, update, and delete webinars
- Users can register or cancel registration
- Admins and hosts can view attendees
- Daily API is used to auto-generate meeting links when needed

---

## Key Design Highlights
- Separation between public access and admin management
- User-level read/unread tracking
- Configurable notification delivery
- Scalable online awareness sessions

---

## Roles Involved
- Public Users
- Patient
- Doctor
